Thank you for visiting my website. My name is John Handley and I have been making non-functional sculptural vessels for about twenty years now.

↓

About my Work!

I produce my work at The Mac Studios in Edgbaston in Birmingham, UK. My influences are based in nature, particularly rural and coastal landscapes ravaged by erosion. I am passionate about the elements in nature creating random shapes and beautiful colours. This is reflected in the muted blue/green shades and rough texture of my work. I construct my forms in an unusual way, initially throwing the basic shape on a wheel, then turning and sculpting the desired shape by hand. This method is very time consuming but enjoyable and rewarding.
